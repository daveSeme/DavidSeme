Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury safari experiences.

What is the best time of year to go on safari in Kenya?

The dry season (June to October) is generally considered the best time for wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. However, the wet season (November to May) offers lush landscapes, newborn animals, and excellent bird watching opportunities. We offer exceptional experiences year-round.

What should I pack for my luxury safari?

We recommend neutral-colored clothing, a wide-brimmed hat, high-quality sunscreen, premium binoculars, a professional camera with extra batteries, and comfortable walking shoes. Evenings can be cool, so bring a light jacket or sweater. All our luxury lodges provide high-end amenities, so you can travel light.

Are children allowed on your luxury safaris?

Yes, we welcome children aged 8 and above on our safaris. We offer family-friendly luxury packages with adjusted itineraries, child-friendly activities, and private guides to ensure an enriching experience for all ages. Some lodges also offer childcare services.

What safety measures are in place during the safari?

All our guides are highly trained in first aid and wildlife safety. Our luxury vehicles are equipped with satellite communication devices, and we follow strict park regulations. We maintain small group sizes for personalized attention and safety. Your wellbeing is our utmost priority throughout your journey.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before departure receive a full refund. Between 60-90 days, we offer a 75% refund. Between 30-60 days, we offer a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot refund cancellations made within 30 days of departure, but we can help reschedule your luxury safari experience.
